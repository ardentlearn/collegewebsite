About Us

Indore Institute of Science &Technology (IIST) is established in the year 2003, is amongst the Top 5 Engineering Colleges of Indore. The Engineering Institute is approved by All India Institute of Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and affiliated to RGPV Bhopal, offering Bachelor’s degree in CSE, IT, EC, Civil, Chemical and Mechanical Engineering and Master’s Degree in CSE, Machine Design and Digital Communication. The group’s Pharmacy Institute (IIP) is approved by AICTE and Pharmacy Council of India (PCI), New Delhi, Affiliated to RGPV Bhopal, offering Diploma (D. Pharm), Undergraduate (B. Pharm) and Post Graduate programs (M. Pharm in Pharmaceutics & Quality Assurance). The group’s Management Institute (IIMR) is approved by AICTE, Delhi; Department of Technical Education and Department of Higher Education, Government of M.P. and is affiliated to Devi Ahilya Vishwa Vidhyalaya, Indore, offering MBA, BBA - Foreign Trade, BBA - Plain, B.Com (Hons.), BSc (Computer Science, Maths and Statistics), B.A.(Hons.).

At IIST group of institutions, we are committed to enhancing the employability Quotient of the students with Holistic Development. Towards this objective, we have dedicated Special Interest Groups (SIGs) on emerging areas apart from 23 clubs for extracurricular activities, games & sports. The various SIGs focus on the Capacity building of the faculty and students and enable them to take up the activities related to training, research and development in the emerging fields. The Career Development Cell (CDC) helps in enhancing the Aptitude and Soft Skills of the students in order to strengthen them to get better career opportunity. The Placement, Entrepreneurship and Career Advancement (PECA) Cell helps them in activities other than placements like Entrepreneurship Development or equipping themselves for higher studies etc. IIST group of Institutes has a very good Industry-Academia tie ups to bridge the gap between the requirements of the Industry and university driven regular curriculum, through International/National Certification courses.

Code of Competence and emphasis on quality at every level right from the Institute canteen (wherein we provide organic vegetables freshly grown in our agricultural fields under Green Waves Club) to class room teaching by adopting best practices, is our prime motto.

Indore Institute Of Science & Technology

Indore Institute of Science &Technology (IIST) established in the year 2003, is amongst the Top 5 Engineering Colleges of Indore. The Institute is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to RGPV Bhopal offering Bachelor’s degree in CS, IT, EC, Civil, Chemical and Mechanical Engineering and Master’s Degree in CS, Machine Design and Digital Communication.

At IIST,we are committed to enhance the employability Quotient of the Students along with Holistic Development. Towards this we have dedicated Special Interest Groups (SIGs) on emerging areas like Data Analytics, Cloud Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things (IoT), Robotics, Mechatronics, Additive Manufacturing, Renewable Energy, Sustainable Development, etc. These SIGs focus on the Capacity building of the faculty and students and enable them to take up the activities related to training, research and development on the emerging fields.

IIST is also associated with IITs like IIT Bombay for e-yantra Robotics Lab, Spoken Tutorial and IIT Delhi for Virtual Lab and also have good Industry-Academia tie ups like the AWS Academy, RedHat Academy, Microsoft Imagine Academy, Facebook Developer Circles, Google Developer Student Clubs, MSME, etc. to bridge the gap between the requirements of the Industry and the curriculum through International/ National Certificate courses.

Dedicated and highly experienced team of faculty member. Availability of e-library facility and online teaching platforms to train the students round the clock. An integrative and multidisciplinary approach to ensure quality education. Individual mentoring of students via syndicate groups (20-25 students) by faculty.

All the year-round training for Soft Skills and Personality Development from the first semester. Add-on certificate courses/SIG activity and Project-based learning in Quality control/Assurance, Clinical Trials, Pharmacovigilance, Pharmaceutical Marketing. Skill-building by offering Industry ready certification through MOOC. 100 % placements of eligible students. Highest package of 12.5 lacks Career Guidance in Pharmacy profession with the help of dedicated Career Development Cell and Placement Cell.

Best in class infrastructure which includes well-equipped laboratories, smart classrooms, Seminar hall, etc. Convenient location at the interface of City and SEZ Pithampur, enabling good industrial exposure. Finest sports facility in Madhya Pradesh which includes Basketball Court, Tennis Court, Volleyball court, etc. Provision of activities by Professional Bodies like Pharmacy Council of India, Indian Pharmacist Association, and Rotary Club, etc.

The student who comes to study management hails from diversified fields of study and has to work in all sorts and types of diversity. IIMR is offering best of mba in indore and has built the process and practices for developing all the requisites of successful managers like communication and soft skill development, association with Industry for training and exposure, creative and innovative research, teaching based on modern pedagogy and an excellence driven culture.
